Ishaan + Nikumbh

Nikumbh saw something in Ishaan that no one else was looking for. Ishaan learned to trust that someone could see him. Between them they know what it means to be failed by a system — and what it means to be truly seen by one person.

  • The teacher tore my daughter's map of an imaginary country in front of the class. She hasn't spoken in 5 days.
    Suresh, Mandya
  • My 13-year-old stole from the classmates who have been bullying him for two years. The principal says bullying is "no excuse."
    Deepak, Nagpur
  • I'm 16. I've always been the bad student in a family of engineers. I just realised I understand everything when it's explained through music. No one at school teaches anything that way.
    Arjun, Dehradun
  • My son recites cricket commentary by heart but cannot write a sentence without crying.
    Priya, Patna
  • I am 7. The geography didi shouted at me for sharing a pencil. Mummy asks for homework first, food second. I want to know why school feels like a test every day.
    Shirin, Mumbai
  • I am a young teacher with a student who thinks in a different language than my classroom allows.
    Fatima, Srinagar
