3 Idiots

Rancho + Raju

Rancho has never once done what was expected of him. Raju has never once done what he actually wanted. Together they're slowly figuring out that both extremes are wrong — but Rancho's usually more right.

  • I'm not asking anything. I just play guitar in my car for 30 minutes before going home.
    Ranjit, Jorhat
  • I have a bank job and I do pottery on the terrace at 6am — am I ungrateful?
    Vivek, Mangaluru
  • My father chose B.Com for me. I want geography. Is it even a real career?
    Sarita, Allahabad
  • I came back from London for my father in 2014 and have not made one decision on my own since.
    Siddharth, Delhi
  • My son wants to drop out of engineering for Hindustani classical music. I keep saying "do what makes you happy." My face does not say that.
    Amitabha, Kolkata
  • Good MBA with ₹18 lakh loan, or a permanent government job?
    Lakshmi, Coimbatore
  • My father tells the relatives I am studying “communications.” I am studying journalism. I have not corrected him. I am quietly building a portfolio.
    Fatima, Hyderabad
