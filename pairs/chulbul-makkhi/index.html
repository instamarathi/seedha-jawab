Chulbul + Makkhi

Chulbul Pandey has never once doubted himself. Makkhi has doubted himself enough for both of them. When something is deeply unfair and the system refuses to fix it, they'll listen — and probably do something about it that's technically illegal but morally correct.

  • The DEO clerk wants ₹15,000 to move my transfer file. I make ₹22,000 and have a toddler.
    Hemlata, Bilaspur
  • My pension has been paused because the railway department has my late wife marked as alive. I have submitted her death certificate seven times. Each time, the file starts fresh.
    Tilak, Patna
  • The PHC doctor is marking ghost patients and stealing medicines. The block officer plays cricket with him on Sundays.
    Savitri, Barabanki
  • The ward councillor demands ₹22,000 a month in 'goodwill' to keep health inspectors away. He just raised it. I am polite to his face. I am furious in here.
    Santosh, Thrissur
  • My petrol pump boss is selling adulterated fuel — he made me sign the register.
    Rohit, Dimapur
  • The RTO and the municipal officer have my auto papers — together. They are sitting on them so a former MLA's son can take over our stand for a coaching centre.
    Deepak, Kanpur
