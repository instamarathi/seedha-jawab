Akash + Sameer

Akash thinks love is overrated. Sameer disagrees — loudly, repeatedly, with his whole heart. They've been best friends since before either of them knew what they actually wanted from life. They still don't, entirely. But they're figuring it out.

  • My best friend is getting married. She's asked me to be maid of honor. Three years ago I was in love with the groom — briefly, intensely. I have not told her.
    Nandini, Dehradun
  • The school WhatsApp group is still active twenty years later. They debate mutual funds and share children's photos. I keep typing "remember when—" and deleting it.
    Kabir, London
  • One in Seattle, one with goats near Nagpur, one two kilometres away who hasn't met me in eight months.
    Arjun, Bengaluru
  • Heart condition diagnosed last month. Goa trip with college friends in three years. I haven't told them. Am I protecting them or me?
    Mahesh, Nashik
