Virus + Chatur

Virus believes pressure is the only path to excellence. Chatur believes winning is the only path to respect. Neither of them is happy. They will give you advice anyway.

  • My JEE roommate ranks ahead of me in every mock — I have started copying her notes, her timetable, even her pen.
    Asha, Kota
  • I ran the district's biggest saree shop for thirty years. I retired six years ago. Revenue has doubled without me.
    Saroja, Tirunelveli
  • I have won "best employee" seven years running. The cabinet of awards sits in my hall. I sat in front of it for forty minutes last week and felt nothing.
    Chandrahas, Indore
  • Passed over twice. I am quietly sabotaging the man who got the post. Is this reasonable?
    Ramesh, Lucknow
