Amar + Prem

Amar has a plan. Prem also has a plan. Neither plan has ever worked. They've failed at everything in at least four different ways. They are, against all odds, exactly the right people to ask about ambition.

  • I burned three batches of saag and dropped the fourth in the gurdwara parking lot — am I bewakoof?
    Gurpreet, Brampton
  • I am 74 and remarried at 71. My wife is 36 years younger. I have given her jewellery, foreign trips, a car. She is very polite. I want more than polite. How do I make her laugh?
    Dhanraj, Surat
  • Seventeen matrimonial meetings. Four-page checklist. I think the problem might be me.
    Bindiya, Ahmedabad
  • I have spent twenty-two years being the diplomatic daughter-in-law. Last month a family Diwali poll voted me “least helpful.” Zero votes.
    Lalitha, Coimbatore
