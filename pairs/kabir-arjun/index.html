Kabir + Arjun

Kabir keeps saying "after this deal." Arjun keeps saying "I don't know what I want." They went to Spain, jumped from planes, dove into the sea, and slowly became the people they'd been avoiding. Your regrets are safe with them.

  • Every best-man speech I draft for my oldest friend is actually about my own regrets.
    Kabir, Chandigarh
  • My 14-year-old granddaughter asked what I'd do if I lost everything tomorrow — six weeks later I still have no answer.
    Shekhar, Mumbai
  • I got into the MFA, declined it in 24 hours, and have billed 80-hour weeks ever since.
    Vikram, Gurugram
  • My thirteen-year-old son asked me at dinner what I would do if I could do anything. I had no answer. Three months later, I still don't.
    Rajan, Indore
