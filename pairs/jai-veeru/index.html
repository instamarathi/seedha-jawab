Jai + Veeru

Jai doesn't say much. Veeru doesn't stop talking. They've had each other's backs since before either of them remembers why. If your friendship is cracking at the seams, they've been there.

  • My oldest friend moved to Canada and his children don't know my name.
    Mustafa, Kolkata
  • My oldest friend and I stopped speaking after the 2019 elections — I don't want politics, I want him back.
    Ashraf, Lucknow
  • A friendship of forty years ended over a casteist comment my friend made — casually, as if she had always thought it. I have not called her since. I am not sure I should.
    Rukmini, Mysuru
  • My oldest friend got the promotion I was passed over for — and we still meet for chai on Tuesdays.
    Manvendra, Chandigarh
