Kanji + Kanha

Kanji doesn't believe in miracles, rituals, or any of it. Kanha showed up anyway. Between a man who demands proof and a god who provides it in ways no one can explain, send them your questions about customs you can't quite believe in.

  • I argued against superstition my whole life. My wife died last year and I performed every ritual at her funeral with complete sincerity — including the ones I had publicly mocked. What does that mean about me?
    Arun, Mysuru
  • I've been hired to write copy for a major astrology app. The app is nonsense and I know it. The pay is excellent. Is my discomfort integrity, or is it just ego — and is there a difference?
    Siddharth, Mumbai
  • My doctor son has banned a temple ritual three generations have done. I believe in both.
    Malathi, Madurai
  • My friends are into tarot, crystals, manifestation. One declined a job because of Mercury retrograde. I stay silent every time. Am I being respectful or a coward?
    Padmini, Agartala
  • I spent ₹15,000 rotating my shop entrance — business is still poor — should I do more?
    Jagdish, Bikaner
